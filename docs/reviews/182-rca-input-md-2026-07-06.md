## Findings

### 1. [Medium] ambiguity · high confidence — Fix approach, item 2

> `steps.md` Step 14: gate `gh issue close` — close directly only if the PR merged
> during this run (Step 12 executed); otherwise DO NOT close, relying on the
> `(closes #N)` linkage to auto-close on merge.

**Ambiguity:** The artifact does not define whether Step 12 execution always means a successful merge.
