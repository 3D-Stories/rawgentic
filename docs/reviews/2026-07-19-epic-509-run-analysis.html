Where to cut it — three moves

All recover time without weakening the gates that caught the real defects.

1 · Page the owner at review-verdict-ready (or arm the resume launcher)

The one 56-minute gap sat between a reviewer verdict landing and the owner's “resume” — 18% of total wall, more than any phase average. A notify-owner push at verdict-ready (or the epic509-resume.sh launcher armed at run start) removes it entirely.

−56mper attended run

2 · Scope the pre-PR re-run after prose-only review fixes

5 of 9 children re-ran the full suite (~2.4 min each) because a review fix landed after Step 9 — usually a guard-pinned prose tweak. Tightening §12's re-run trigger to the affected guard subset keeps the gate and drops the ceremony.

−11mper epic

3 · Script the new-skill count-surface walk

#508's implement ran 18.6 min vs the 8.9-min median — the delta is the registration walk across whitelist, symlink, sync, canary, and six count strings, plus a full-suite round-trip to find the second hand-pinned count in test_interview_skill.py. A helper that prints every stale surface makes this mechanical.

−4mper new skill
