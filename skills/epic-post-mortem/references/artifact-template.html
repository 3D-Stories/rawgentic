The honest part: the review depth is not the waste.

Those 9 reviewers per child caught a ~93-minute ReDoS, a child-FIFO teardown DoS, a silent CAS revert, and an empirically-broken setsid pane command — real Critical/High defects that would have shipped. Cutting review means shipping those. The win is not idling while it runs, not re-running the full suite five times, and using the right model tier — not reviewing less. Speed from process, not from lowering the bar.
